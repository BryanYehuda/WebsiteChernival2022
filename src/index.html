Chemical Engineering Innovation Festival 2022

“Discharging innovation through youth collaboration”

Chernival is an annual series of events which in this year consists of National OlymPiad and innovation challEnge of Chemical engineering (NOPEC) 2022, Indonesia Chemical Reaction Car Competition (ICRCC) 2021, and Closing Event Chernival which consists CHERNIVAL Got Talent & CHERNIVAL Bazaar. Chernival is the biggest agenda by the students of Chemical Engineering Department FT-IRS ITS. With the goal to participate in humanity, we hope to fulfill it by holding series of events on Chernival 2021.





NOPEC


January-February

National OlymPiad and innovation challEnge of Chemical engineering (NOPEC) is one of the biggest competition in Indonesia which is held for senior high school students who are curious about Chemical Engineering and want to challenge themselves to aim higher.

ICRCC


March-May

Indonesia Chemical Reaction Car Competition is Reaction Car competition with objective is to create car prototype that could be run by chemical reaction through the specified distance while carrying specified load. And also poster presentation competition and many more additional competition.

Closing Event


April-June

The closing event of CHERNIVAL 2022 in this year consists of CHERNIVAL Got Talent & CHERNIVAL Bazaar. This event aims to introduce the Department of Chemical Engineering FT-IRS ITS to the public through several series of cool events.
