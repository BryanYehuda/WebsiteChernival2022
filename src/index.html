Chemical Engineering Innovation Festival 2022

“Discharging Innovation through Youth Collaboration”

Explore More

Chemical Engineering Innovation Festival (CHERNIVAL) is an annual series of events which is held by Chemical Engineering ITS. With the goal to provide a platform for improving and contributing to the industrial world for rising generations in the national and international scale. This year, CHERNIVAL consists of National Olympiad and Innovation Challenge of Chemical Engineering (NOPEC), Indonesia Chemical Reaction Car Competition (ICRCC), and Closing Event which includes CHERNIVAL Bazaar and Music.





NOPEC


January-February

National Olympiad and Innovation Challenge of Chemical Engineering (NOPEC) is one of the biggest competition in Indonesia which is held for senior high school students who are curious about Chemical Engineering and want to challenge themselves to aim higher. Are you ready to become future chemical engineer?

View Details

ICRCC


March-May

Indonesia Chemical Reaction Car Competition (ICRCC) is an international-scaled competition which engages undergraduate student to pour their innovative ideas in developing a prototype car which is powered by chemical energy source that will safely carry a specified load over a given distance and stop. Are you dare to compete in this prestigious competition?

Closing Event


April-June

Closing Event CHERNIVAL 2022 will give a whole new experience for Surabayans to bring back the excitement of strolling around bazaar and enjoying music. Be ready for getting thousands of fun!
