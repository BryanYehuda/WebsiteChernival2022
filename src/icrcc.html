ICRCC 2022

All undergraduate students majoring in Chemical Engineering and Process Engineering, Chemistry, and further courses are invited to contribute their knowledge, creativity, and ability in a team to innovate a new and eco-friendly resources.




Scroll down to learn more about ICRCC 2022

ICRCC (Indonesia Chemical Reaction Car Competition) 2022 is an international competition between universities to use chemical engineering and processes, creativity, and teamwork to design and make cars prototypes driven by chemical reactions that will stop accurately to reach a certain distance.





NOPEC


January-February

National Olympiad and Innovation Challenge of Chemical Engineering (NOPEC) is one of the biggest competition in Indonesia which is held for senior high school students who are curious about Chemical Engineering and want to challenge themselves to aim higher. Are you ready to become future chemical engineer?

View Details

ICRCC


March-May

Indonesia Chemical Reaction Car Competition (ICRCC) is an international-scaled competition which engages undergraduate student to pour their innovative ideas in developing a prototype car which is powered by chemical energy source that will safely carry a specified load over a given distance and stop. Are you dare to compete in this prestigious competition?

Bagaimana cara mendaftarnya?

Langkah-langkah pendaftaran:
1. Daftarkan tim Anda di www.chernival.com (klik “Register”).
2. Setiap tim wajib membayar biaya pendaftaran ke Mandiri 1480019411878 (Cheryn Ayudya Ardhaneswara) dan mengunggah bukti pembayaran.
3. Setiap tim wajib mengunggah hasil scan kartu pelajar atau surat keterangan pengganti kartu pelajar.
4. Tunggu verifikasi (maksimal 2 x 24 jam).
5. Bergabung dengan grup Telegram (link undangan grup Telegram akan diberikan setelah proses pendaftaran dan pembayaran selesai).
